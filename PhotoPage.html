Here is a little gallery of NieR Automata concept art that I like

2B

This is concept art of 2B. She is one of the three playable protaganists of the NieR Automata game. She is a YoRHa android to fight the machine lifeforms that are currently infesting earth.

Devola & Popola

This image is concept art for Devola & Popola. Devola & Popola are two side charaters in the game that play a crucial role towards the end of the game. They also appeared in the previous game NieR.

Adam & Eve

This image is concept art for Adam & Eve. Adam & Eve are two of the antagonists of the game and are very central in the first playthrough of the game.
